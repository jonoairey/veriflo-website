About Veriflo

We're building the truth layer for digital content. Our mission is to make document fraud impossible and leaks traceable.

Our Mission

In a world where digital documents can be easily forged and leaked, trust is broken. Businesses lose millions to document fraud. Creators lose revenue to piracy. Confidential information gets leaked without consequence.

Veriflo exists to restore trust in digital content. We combine blockchain verification with forensic fingerprinting to create a system where documents are provably authentic and leaks are traceable to their source.

Every document deserves to be trusted. Every creator deserves to protect their work. Every business deserves to know when confidential information is compromised.

Our Story

Veriflo was founded by a team of security researchers, blockchain engineers, and legal professionals who witnessed firsthand the devastating impact of document fraud and leaks.

After seeing a major M&A deal nearly collapse due to leaked documents, and watching content creators lose their livelihoods to piracy, we knew there had to be a better way. Traditional watermarks could be removed. DLP systems cost six figures and still didn't work. Legal action was slow and expensive.

We built Veriflo to solve both problems at once: proving documents are authentic through blockchain verification, and identifying leakers through invisible forensic fingerprinting that survives any manipulation.

Today, we're protecting millions of documents for businesses, creators, and institutions worldwide. Our technology has been used as evidence in court cases, recovered millions in damages, and prevented countless fraudulent transactions.
