Pricing FAQs

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges.

What happens if I exceed my document limit?

We'll notify you when you're close to your limit. You can either upgrade your plan or purchase additional documents at $2 per document.

Is there a long-term contract?

No contracts required for monthly plans. Annual plans lock in your rate for 12 months but can be cancelled with 30 days notice.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and ACH bank transfers for Enterprise plans.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?

Yes! We offer 50% discounts for qualified nonprofit organizations and educational institutions. Contact us for verification.

What's included in the Free tier forever?

The Free tier never expires and includes 3 documents per month with basic watermarking and blockchain verification. It's perfect for trying the technology.

How does Enterprise pricing work?

Enterprise pricing is customized based on your volume, feature requirements, and SLA needs. Contact our sales team for a detailed quote tailored to your organization.
