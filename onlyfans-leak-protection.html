Creator FAQ

Will subscribers know their content is fingerprinted?

No. The fingerprints are completely invisible - there's no visible difference between fingerprinted and regular content. Your subscribers will never notice anything different about the quality or appearance of your photos and videos.

What if someone screenshots my content?

Our fingerprints survive screenshots! Unlike visible watermarks that can be cropped, our 5-layer technology embeds markers at the pixel level that persist through screenshots, screen recordings, compression, and even social media re-uploads.

How do I integrate with OnlyFans?

Upload your content to Veriflo first. We'll create fingerprinted copies for each subscriber. Then you share the specific copy with each subscriber via OnlyFans DMs or mass messages. We also support Fansly, Patreon, and other platforms.

Can I actually sue a leaker?

Yes! Our forensic reports are designed to be court-admissible evidence. We provide detailed chain-of-custody documentation that proves which specific person received the leaked copy. Several creators have successfully pursued legal action using Veriflo evidence.

What's included in the $29/month plan?

The Starter plan includes: 50 protected files per month, full 5-layer fingerprinting, leak detection alerts, QR verification codes, bulk upload capability, and email support. Perfect for individual creators just getting started with content protection.
