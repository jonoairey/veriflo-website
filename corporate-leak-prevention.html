Enterprise FAQ

Can we deploy on-premises?

Yes. For enterprises with strict data sovereignty requirements, we offer on-premises deployment options. Documents never leave your infrastructure. Contact our enterprise team to discuss your requirements.

How does SSO integration work?

We support SAML 2.0 integration with all major identity providers including Okta, Azure AD, OneLogin, and Ping Identity. Users authenticate through your existing identity provider - no separate passwords needed.

What's included in enterprise pricing?

Enterprise pricing includes unlimited documents, SSO/SAML, API access, white-labeling, custom integrations, dedicated account manager, 24/7 priority support, and SLA guarantee. Pricing is customized based on your organization's size and needs.

Can we run a pilot program first?

Absolutely. We offer enterprise pilot programs typically lasting 30-90 days. This allows you to test with your specific use cases and workflows before committing. Contact our sales team to discuss pilot scope.
