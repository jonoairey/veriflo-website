The Problem We Solve

Document Fraud

Document fraud costs businesses $133 billion annually. Fake invoices, forged credentials, and altered contracts erode trust in every industry.

  • Invoice fraud up 65% since 2020
  • Credential fraud affecting 1 in 4 hires
  • Contract disputes from altered documents

Confidential Leaks

The average data breach costs $4.88 million. Insider threats and document leaks destroy careers, companies, and trust relationships.

  • 60% of breaches involve insiders
  • 277 days average to identify breach source
  • Leakers operate with impunity

Our Solution

Dual-purpose protection in one platform

Blockchain Verification

Every document gets a cryptographic hash written to the Polygon blockchain, creating an immutable timestamp that proves the document existed and was unaltered at a specific moment.

  • Tamper-evident verification
  • QR code instant checking
  • Legal certificates

Forensic Fingerprinting

Our proprietary 5-layer fingerprinting embeds invisible markers in every copy. If content leaks, we identify exactly which recipient's copy was shared - with 98.5% accuracy.

  • Survives screenshots & edits
  • Completely invisible
  • Court-admissible evidence
